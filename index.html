Our Services

  • Literature Review Support: Find and summarize relevant papers, create annotated bibliographies.
  • Data Annotation: Label fMRI, EEG, DTI images or spreadsheets.
  • Citation Cleanup: Format citations (APA/MLA), fix Mendeley/Zotero libraries.
  • AI-Aided Drafting: Abstracts, summaries, slide content, and lay summaries powered by GPT.
  • File & Data Organization: Clean and sort research folders and rename datasets.

How It Works

  1. Tell us what you need via email or form.
  2. We assign a student researcher based on your task.
  3. We deliver within your timeframe — fast, accurate, and affordable.
